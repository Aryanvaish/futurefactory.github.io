OVERVIEW

In the era of Smart Manufacturing - 'Industry 4.0' or 'Intelligent Factory', the power of technology is being realized across the industrial world and radically transforming the manufacturing ecosystem. The recent innovations and breakthroughs across the industries are bridging digital goals for the next generation of manufacturing excellence.

From big data to cloud computing, the benefits of modern technologies are helping manufacturers enhance the speed of operations, streamline the manufacturing process, reduce human intervention and minimize errors in the process. Besides, technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning and additive manufacturing are helping them ensure efficiency, reduce waste and increase plant productivity and overall profitability.

SECTORS COVERED

AUTOMOTIVE

14th September 2022

The automotive sector is one of the most enthusiastic sectors in terms of making huge investments and setting higher targets for its digital manufacturing operations compared to other industries. However, in a bid to exploit the full potential of smart factories, Auto OEMs and suppliers will have to address a number of priorities, from a comprehensive strategy to integrated solutions, leverage IoT to talent acquisition. Besides, the sector needs to address several challenges including the cost of connectivity, complex supply chain, and technological capabilities among others to accomplish India's 'factory to the world' vision.
